### 3. Real Training vs. Token Training

**Finding**: Union contract requires "real training" not "token training".

**Real Training Definition** (Jimmy):
- $50K per person (not $5K token)
- Months-long programs (not 2-day workshops)
- Dedicated instructors, hands-on practice
- Paid time (regular wage while training)
- Fair opportunity to learn

**Impact**:
- If 20-30 workers need retraining: $1-1.5M (NOT $100-150K)
- Budget shortfall: $900K-1.35M unbudgeted
- Article 15 (seniority) prevents external hiring until existing workers trained first
- If training inadequate: Cannot staff cloud roles, migration blocked

**Action Required**:
- Budget realistic training ($50K/person)
- Design WITH union (what does "real training" look like?)
- Pilot program with volunteers
- Document good-faith effort for contract compliance
