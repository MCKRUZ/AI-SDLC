Critical Contradictions (8)

CONTRADICTION 1: Timeline Expectations [CRITICAL]

Michael Chen (CEO): "18 months total, 90-day quick wins"

Stakeholder Consensus (Sarah, Jennifer, Robert, David, Patricia, Maria, Alex): "36-60 months minimum, infrastructure prerequisites alone are 6-9 months before first migration"

Mathematical Proof (Alex, Maria): Sunday windows = 48 hours/year available = 3-5 years minimum for 8 systems

Gap: 200-300% timeline underestimate

Source: All 8 interviews

Impact: If 18-month timeline mandated, project will fail catastrophically

Resolution Required:

  • Board must approve 36-60 month realistic timeline
  • Michael must present mathematical proof to board
  • PE firm must accept multi-year investment cycle
  • Quick wins redefined as pilot programs, not production migrations

Resolution Dependency: Must be resolved before budget approval (Contradiction #10)

CONTRADICTION 10: Total Project Cost [CRITICAL]

Michael's Implication: $3-5M total

Jennifer's Initial Estimate: $3-5M over 3-5 years

Discovery Reality (After Interview 8): $18.5-26.5M total

Primary Driver - Training Costs:

  • Maria (MES Supervisor): $9M for 180 MES operators alone
  • Linda (HR): $12-15M total training across all roles
  • This is 60-65% of total project budget
  • Not in original budget model at all

Gap: 617-883% budget underestimate

Source: Interviews 3 (Jennifer), 8 (Maria), 6 (Linda)

Impact: Board/PE approval required for $18.5-26.5M investment

Resolution Required:

  • Complete financial model with all costs visible
  • Board approval for $18.5-26.5M investment
  • PE firm acceptance of Year 1-2 cost increases
  • Training budget secured ($12-15M)
  • Support contractor budget secured ($2-3M)

CONTRADICTION 7: Training Capacity & Cost [CRITICAL]

Original Assumption: $300-500K training budget (Jennifer's estimate)

Discovery Reality: $12-15M training budget required

Per-Role Breakdown:

  • MES Operators: $9M (180 × $50K) - Maria Interview
  • IT Team: $1.2-1.5M (12-month Azure training)
  • Support Team: $500-750K
  • Floor Supervisors: $750K-1M (15 supervisors)
  • Quality/Compliance: $300-500K

Gap: 2400-5000% training cost underestimate

Source: Interview 8 (Maria), Interview 6 (Linda)

Resolution Required:

  • Secure $12-15M training budget
  • Source manufacturing-experienced trainers (4-6 month lead time)
  • Design 4-phase training program
  • Accept 18-24 month training delivery timeline
