Top 5 Critical Themes

THEME 1: SAP Trauma as Organizational PTSD

Frequency: 7 of 8 interviews

Evidence: Michael (awareness), Sarah (IT trauma), Jennifer (financial trauma), Robert (floor trauma), Maria (3 weeks chaos), Patricia (validation failure), Alex (infrastructure trauma)

Key Insights:

  • 2018 SAP failure created lasting trauma: 3 weeks of 50% production drop, 3 employees lost, $8M investment with <$500K value
  • 7 years later: "New system" announcement = resume updates
  • Floor operators: "We're not doing that again" is universal response
  • This is not ancient history - this is active organizational PTSD

Impact on Project:

  • Cannot ignore or minimize SAP failure
  • Must explicitly acknowledge what went wrong
  • Must demonstrate learning and different approach
  • Trust must be rebuilt through partnership, not mandates
