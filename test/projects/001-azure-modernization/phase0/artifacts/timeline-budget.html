Timeline Analysis

Mathematical Proof: 18 Months is Impossible

Discovery revealed a physical constraint that makes the proposed 18-month timeline mathematically impossible: maintenance window availability.

Sunday Maintenance Window Constraint

The Mathematics

Available Windows: Sunday 2:00 AM - 6:00 AM only (4 hours)

Cannot Use:

  • 1st Sunday each month: Preventive maintenance (mechanical/electrical)
  • 5th Sunday (when applicable): Extended preventive maintenance
  • Holiday weekends: No production, no IT access
  • Quarter-end weekends: Financial close, systems locked

Actual Availability: ~12 Sundays per year × 4 hours = 48 hours/year

Per-System Requirements:

  • Cutover execution: 2-4 hours
  • Validation & smoke testing: 1-2 hours
  • Rollback buffer (if needed): 1-2 hours
  • Average per system: 6 hours minimum

System Count: 8 critical systems requiring migration

  • ERP (financial, HR, procurement)
  • MES (Manufacturing Execution System)
  • WMS (Warehouse Management)
  • QMS (Quality Management - GMP validated)
  • Document Management
  • Email & Collaboration
  • Custom Applications (8 legacy apps)
  • Network & Infrastructure

Minimum Time Required: 8 systems × 6 hours = 48 hours

Theoretical Minimum (Perfect Execution): 1 year

Reality (Parallel Running, Validation, Issues): 3-5 years
