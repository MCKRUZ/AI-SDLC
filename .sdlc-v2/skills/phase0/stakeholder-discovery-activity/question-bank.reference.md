# Interview Question Bank

## Opening Questions

- "Walk me through a typical day in your role."
- "What's the most frustrating part of your current process?"
- "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing, what would it be?"

## Pain Point Exploration

- "Tell me about the last time [problem] happened."
- "How often does this occur?"
- "What's the impact when it happens?"
- "What do you do to work around it?"

## Root Cause (Five Whys Starters)

- "Why do you think that happens?"
- "What causes that situation?"
- "Has it always been this way?"

## Jobs-to-be-Done

- "What are you ultimately trying to accomplish?"
- "How do you measure success for this task?"
- "What would 'done' look like?"

## Constraints and Politics

- "What can't change, no matter what?"
- "Who else would need to approve changes here?"
- "What's been tried before?"
- "Why didn't that work?"

## Forward-Looking

- "If this project succeeds, what does that look like in 6 months?"
- "What would make you consider this a failure?"
- "What keeps you up at night about this initiative?"

## Closing

- "What haven't I asked that I should have?"
- "Who else should I talk to?"
- "Can I follow up if I have more questions?"
